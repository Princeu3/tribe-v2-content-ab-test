What if you could test how someone's brain reacts to your content before they ever see it? No focus groups. No A/B tests. No waiting for analytics. Meta just made that real. They open sourced TRIBE v2, a model that predicts human brain activity from any video, audio, or text. Trained on seven hundred brains. Eleven hundred hours of scans. Seventy thousand data points per prediction. I ran this post through it before publishing. The version that lit up your temporal and prefrontal cortex the most? That's the one you're reading right now.
